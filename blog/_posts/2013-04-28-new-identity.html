Although I love cubes, my old logo didn't quite say much about me.

Old Identity

The cube was pretty generic and not all that impressive.

New Identity

While browsing for inspiration I stumbled the icaras font and very much liked the blocky style it has.

I then created my own variation and gave it a 3D blocky feel.

This was done in Photoshop by setting grid lines at certain points and filling in the rectangles.

The official hex colors that I am using are #2D3237 for the dark gray background, and #0088CC for the aqua blue.

M for Moogs™

Before I was using my own name to promote myself, but now I'm shifting to using Moogs™, LLC. (moogs.io) as my company name.

It makes me seem less egoistic. Maybe?
