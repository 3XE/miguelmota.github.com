About me

Howdy, I'm Miguel Mota.

I specialize in HTML5/CSS3, Javascript(jQuery) and PHP/SQL. Currently I am a mobile web developer for XTOPOLY, Inc. and at the same time attending Norco College getting my transter studies completed, and then transferring to CSUF to major in computer science.

I am a 21 year old from the always sunny Southern California, with 3 years of experience in doing what I do. I love to code as much I love to watch videos of flying lawnmowers. I live for nutella sammiches and strawberry milk.

To say hello reach me at hello@miguelmota.com

About the site

This is my playground where I experiment with new findings. You will find here my lifestream and a showcase of my work. Every once in a while I will blog a tutorial related to all things web in which I found interesting.

The site is powered by jekyll, a static-site generator, and github pages. The icons that you see are from the Pictos icon set created by the talented Drew Wilson. The font is from the Ubuntu font family.
